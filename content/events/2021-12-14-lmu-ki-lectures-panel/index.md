  <p>LMU asked two of its scholars and one graduate to share with the public their insights and their approaches to the ethical challenges of artificial intelligence as part of the AI Lectures.</p>

Moderating the discussion will be Martin Wirsing, Professor of Computer Science at LMU and a sought-after expert in the field of programming, software engineering and development.</p>
