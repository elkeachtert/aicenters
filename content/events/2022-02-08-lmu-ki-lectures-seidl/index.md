  <p>In his lecture, Thomas Seidl, Professor of Computer Science at LMU, will talk about the latest developments in artificial intelligence.   <p>In addition, Professor Seidl will offer insights into research at the Munich Center for Machine Learning. He will discuss the methodological foundations and the development of new technologies in image and speech recognition.
