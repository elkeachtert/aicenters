  <p>The 5th edition of the European Workshop on Uncertainty in Machine Learning
  (WUML) is organized by our PI Eyke Hüllermeier and his team.</p><p>The goal of

  uncertainty in machine learning. It is meant to provide a forum for discussing
  the most recent developments in the modeling, processing, and quantification
  of uncertainty in machine learning problems, and the exploration of new
  research directions in this field.</p><p>Like the previous editions, the
  workshop is an informal event without a paper submission system. Every
  participant has the choice to give a talk, present a poster, or just attend
  the workshop without a presentation.</p>
