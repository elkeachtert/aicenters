The research foci of MCML are divided into three areas 

While the findings from the research focus “Foundations of Machine Learning” form the basis for methodological advances in ML, the research fields in the area of “Perception, Vision, and Natural Language Processing” represent key-enabling technologies for a variety of real-world applications, e.g., autonomous driving. The research focus “Domain-Specific Machine Learning” combines expertise from the fields of medicine, biology, physics, geosciences, and social and human sciences. In close exchange with the two other areas, ML methods are developed for application-related and socially relevant problems.
