<p>MCML is a joint initiative of leading researchers of Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München and Technische Universität München.</p>
<p>MCML is established with permanent institutional funding from the Federal Ministry of Education and Research and the State of Bavaria.</p><p>Our center currently consists of 11 research groups in the fields of application-oriented Machine Learning and foundational research. More than 50 Principal Investigators and over 200 Junior Members conduct scientific research in these fields.</p>` >}}
