{{< txtTwoCol-md title="Your qualifications" txt1="<ul><li>Master’s degree or equivalent in one of the following fields (must be completed no later 1 July 2024): informatics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, mathematics, statistics, data science, or physics</li><li>Excellent grades</li><li>A very good command of the English language</li><li>Strong scientific motivation</li><li>Interest in interdisciplinary research</li></ul>" link="" linktext="" img="" >}}
