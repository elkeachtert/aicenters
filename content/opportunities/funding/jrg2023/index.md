As a JRG group leader, you will develop an original research project in a field related to one of the three research areas of MCML: Foundations of Machine Learning; Perception, Vision, and NLP; and Domain-Specific Machine Learning. You will lead a research group for which you are expected to hire two PhD students (both TV-L E 13), who will be funded by MCML.

<p>The call for MCML JRG Leader positions <b>is now open</b>.</p>
<p>We look forward to hearing from you and reviewing your application.</p>

### Intended Start Date

October 1st, 2023 

### Qualifications

* Outstanding PhD in the area of informatics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, mathematics, statistics, data science, or physics
* Outstanding track record, demonstrated by high-impact publications in top venues of machine learning or related fields 
* Excellent communication skills, fluency in English (written and oral) 
* Experience in mentoring M.Sc. or B.Sc. students / running a research team 

### Application deadline 
June 15th, 2023

### Required Documents


* Motivation letter (1-2 pages) 
* Academic CV including Google Scholar link 
* Research project proposal (3-4 pages)
* Names and contact information of two references
