In the opening session, **Heyo Kroemer** (CEO, Charité – Universitätsmedizin Berlin), **Geraldine Rauch** (President, TU Berlin) and **Klaus-Robert Müller** (Co-Director, BIFOLD) emphasized the strategic importance of AI, cross-disciplinary collaboration and Berlin’s role in digital innovation. BIFOLD, one of Germany’s AI competence centers and a key partner in our network, played a central part in shaping the event.

MCML Director [**Daniel Rückert**](/research/groups/rueckert/) delivered the first keynote **“AI and the Future of Medicine"** outlining how Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, especially deep learning, can drive more personalized diagnostics, improve prognoses and better predict treatment responses.
