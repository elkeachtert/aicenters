Hosted by DFKI, researchers, developers, and partners from science and industry were invited to participate in the All Hands Meeting 2025. In workshops, panels, and poster sessions, they discussed current challenges, shared the latest research findings, and developed fresh impetus for future collaborations. The meeting impressively demonstrated how German AI competence centres are joining forces and paving the way for a networked European AI landscape together with the French research community.

Our directors [Thomas Seidl](/research/groups/seidl/) and [Daniel Cremers](/research/groups/cremers/) presented the achievements of MCML from the past year and shared insights into our ongoing research initiatives.
