This hands-on workshop equipped participants with tools and strategies to communicate their research effectively to broader audiences. Together, we explored how science communication can increase visibility, citations, and even open doors to new funding opportunities.

The session focused on:

* Clarifying complex ideas without oversimplifying<br>
* Understanding the role as a communicator and public expert<br>
* Crafting narratives that resonate across different audiences<br>

Throughout the morning, participants practiced communicating their own research and reflected on how to adapt their messages to different audiences.

A **huge thank you** to everyone who joined and contributed to the discussions! Special thanks to **Markus Gottschling** for sharing your deep expertise and for guiding the group with clarity and enthusiasm. It was a real pleasure to welcome both you and **Claudia Brusdeylins** here in Munich!
