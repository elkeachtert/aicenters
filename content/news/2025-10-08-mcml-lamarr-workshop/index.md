teaser: >-
  <p>On September 24th and 25th, the first MCML-LAMARR-Workshop took place at
  the University of Bonn. Main focus was NLP, and its related areas. PIs and
  junior members from both German AI Centers came together to present each
  others research, to be part of networking sessions and to work in discussion
  groups, and to foster engagement in collaboration between our centers. PIs
  Lucie Flek and Mehdi Ali from LAMARR, and JRG Leader <a
  href="/research/groups/hedderich/"><strong>Michael Hedderich</strong></a>,
  alongside many PhD students, gave talks on their research. We all agreed on
  the fact that there is a lot of work to do in NLP with respect to LLM
  finetuning, alignment, and bias and ethics research.</p>
