The workshop brought together researchers and staff to promote gender equality and diversity in all AI competence centers. Following an inspiring talk by Maria Petrova-El Sayed, an expert in women’s networks, participants collaborated in three table discussions: The first, "Networks that Empower: Building a Cross-Center Women's Network,"aimed to strengthen connections, visibility, and support for underrepresented groups in AI, with a focus on women. The second table, "Hiring for Diversity: Designing Diversity-Sensitive Recruitment," explored how to create inclusive recruitment processes that improve diversity among applicants and hires. The third discussion, "Inclusive by Design: Fostering Diversity-Sensitive Teams," focused on building and leading research teams in a diversity-sensitive way.

Laura Wollenweber (BIFOLD) and Alexandra Stang (MCML) moderated the workshop.
