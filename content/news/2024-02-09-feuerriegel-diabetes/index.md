subtitle: LMU Newsroom article
teaser: >-
  <p>Researchers from LMU Munich have developed an AI model that can
  detect low blood sugar levels (hypoglycemia) in people with diabetes while
  they are driving.</p><p>The team around our PI Stefan
  Feuerriegel created a model, that only uses routinely collected driving data
  and head/gaze motion data. The model could be used to develop a real-time
  warning system that could help prevent accidents caused by
  hypoglycemia.</p><p><em>«This study not only showcases the potential
  for AI to improve individual health outcomes but also its role in improving
  safety on public roads.»</em><br />(S. Feuerriegel)</p>
