On October 7th and 8th, the All Hands Meeting 2024 of the German AI competence centers (<a href="https://www.bifold.berlin" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BIFOLD</strong></a>**,** <a href="http://ScaDS.ai" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ScaDS.AI</strong></a>**,** <a href="https://lamarr-institute.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Lamarr Institute</strong></a>**,** <a href="https://tuebingen.ai" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Tübingen AI Center</strong></a>**,** <a href="https://www.dfki.de" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DFKI</strong></a> and **MCML**) took place in Dresden.

Over 200 members of the AI community in Germany, along with national and international guests, gathered at the OSTRA-Dome and OSTRA-Studios. Hosted by ScaDS.AI Dresden/Leipzig, the event aimed to foster networking, build collaborations, and showcase activities in research, education, and service.

<p class="large">We are looking forward to next year!</p>
