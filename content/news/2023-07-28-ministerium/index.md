PStS Mario Brandenburg from the Federal Ministry for Education and Research (BMBF) visited the MCML on his tour covering all national AI competence centers in Germany.

From the Bavarian State Ministry for Science and Art (BayStMWKI, Amtschef Dr. Rolf-Dieter Jungk participated as well as LMU President Bernd Huber and TUM Senior Vice President Gerhard Kramer.

{{< clearFloat >}}

{{< img-float src="/images/news/2023-07-18-pst-brandenburg/2023-07-18-pst-speech.jpg" caption="PStS Mario Brandenburg at MCML" copyright="MCML" alt="PStS Brandenburg" float="right" size="normal" >}}

We presented a vivacious firework of short talks on all areas of MCML’s mission, including fundamental research, junior scientists’ support, and transfer activities.

Our PIs Björn Ommer, Julia Schnabel, Barbara Plank, and Matthias Nießner gave insights to their latest research and presented some highlights of their research results. In addition, our talented PhD-students showcased what they are working on.

Afterwards we enjoyed one-on-one discussions with the guests at the poster reception.
