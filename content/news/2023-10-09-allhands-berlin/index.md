On October 9 and 10, the All Hands Meeting 2023 of the German AI competence centers (<a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="https://www.bifold.berlin">BIFOLD</a>, <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="http://ScaDS.ai">ScaDS.ai</a>, <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="https://lamarr-institute.org">Lamarr Institute</a>, <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="https://tuebingen.ai">Tübingen AI Center</a>, <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" href="https://www.dfki.de">DFKI</a> and MCML) took place in Berlin.

As one of a total of five university competence centers, the MCML accepted the invitation to Berlin to present and exchange current research results on the EUREF campus and to discuss them afterwards.

The event was a success all along the line. It is important to see that Germany is doing AI research from top level and that the state wants to promote and advance this fact. We thank all participants and especially BIFOLD for organizing this great event.

<p class="large">We are looking forward to next year!</p>
