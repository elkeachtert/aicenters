teaser: >-
  <p>On October 9 and 10, the All Hands Meeting 2023 of the German AI competence
  centers (<a href="https://www.bifold.berlin" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener">BIFOLD</a>, <a href="http://ScaDS.ai" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener">ScaDS.AI</a>, <a href="https://lamarr-institute.org"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lamarr Institute</a>, <a
  href="https://tuebingen.ai" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Tübingen AI
  Center</a>, <a href="https://www.dfki.de" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener">DFKI</a> and MCML) took place in Berlin.</p><p>As one of a
  total of five university competence centers, the MCML accepted the invitation
  to Berlin to present and exchange current research results on the EUREF campus
  and to discuss them afterwards.</p><p>The event was a success all along the
  line. It is important to see that Germany is doing AI research from top level
  and that the state wants to promote and advance this fact. We thank all
  participants and especially BIFOLD for organizing this great event.</p>
